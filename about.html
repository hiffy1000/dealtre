Our Story

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Dealtree Solar began as a small team of renewable energy enthusiasts with a vision to make solar power accessible to everyone.

What started as a local initiative has now grown into a leading solar energy provider, serving residential, commercial, and industrial clients across the region.

Our journey has been fueled by innovation, sustainability, and an unwavering commitment to our customers and the planet.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive our commitment to renewable energy

Our Mission

To accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy by providing affordable, reliable, and innovative solar solutions that reduce carbon footprints and energy costs for our customers.

Our Vision

To create a future where clean, renewable energy powers every home and business, fostering energy independence and environmental stewardship for generations to come.
